About the App

AUDIATIONstation is a comprehensive web-based application designed for musicians, music students, and audio enthusiasts. It offers a suite of powerful tools to assist in music production, ear training, and creative exploration.

Core Features

Audio Recorder

A versatile audio recorder with cassette-style interface and modern functionalities.

Pitch Training

Interactive exercises to improve your pitch recognition and vocal accuracy.

Note Detection

Real-time note detection to help you identify musical notes from any audio source.

Ear Training

A dedicated section for ear training exercises to sharpen your musical hearing.

Beatbox Mode

An enhanced beatbox section with a drum machine and vocal recording capabilities.
